# GOROD-POLYMAT
The City of Polymaths
GOROD POLYMAT is Russian for The City of Polymaths, and is a Minsky-style Society of Mind Artificial Intellenge systems.  The name comes from Fred Pohl's Beyond The Blue Even Horizon.  The original GOROD POLYMAT was/will be invested by S. Ya Lavorovna-Broadhead (Essie) the wife of Robinette Broadhead, the main protagonist of the Heechee Saga.
